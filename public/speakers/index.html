About the Guest Speaker Program

Part of the Careers in Code initiative is not only to provide our students with the technical skills they need to become developers but also to teach them the necessary soft skills needed in the professional world. This includes everything from networking to technical and behavorial interview skills. We've found the best way to expose them to such important topics is to bring in industry professionals who are passionate about sharing their wealth of knowledge about their own experiences.

Our Guest Speaker Program was developed with this in mind. It not only enhances our student's abilities but introduces them to the thriving tech community in Syracuse.
